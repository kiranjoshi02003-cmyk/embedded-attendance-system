BIO-SYNC PROJECT: ESP32 R503 FINGERPRINT SENSOR & LCD PINOUT

1. POWER SUPPLY:
   - ESP32 VIN/5V pin  -> R503 Red wire (VCC)
   - ESP32 GND pin      -> R503 Black wire (GND)

2. FINGERPRINT SENSOR (UART COMMUNICATION):
   - ESP32 GPIO 16 (RX2) -> R503 White wire (TX)
   - ESP32 GPIO 17 (TX2) -> R503 Blue wire (RX)
   *Note: Ensure common ground between ESP32 and Sensor.*

3. LCD DISPLAY (I2C COMMUNICATION):
   - ESP32 GPIO 21 (SDA) -> LCD SDA pin
   - ESP32 GPIO 22 (SCL) -> LCD SCL pin
   - ESP32 5V pin        -> LCD VCC pin
   - ESP32 GND pin       -> LCD GND pin
